## Natural Planning Model
1. **Purpose** — why are we doing this?
2. **Vision** — what does wild success look like?
3. **Brainstorm** — what ideas come to mind?
4. **Organize** — group ideas, identify next actions
5. **Next Actions** — what's the very next physical action?

## Jedi Addition (Dorofeev)
- Is this project Proactive (green) or Reactive (red)?
- What energy level does the first next action require?
- Can any actions be delegated immediately?
